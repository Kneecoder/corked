Corked
You had an idea. Corked helps you find out if it's real.
A spark hits and for a moment it feels like everything. Then a day passes. A week. You find it again in your notes app, between two other things you never did anything with either. Not because the idea was bad. Because nothing happened to it.

A spark left in a notes app doesn't age, it rots. No questions reach it. No pressure tests it. It just sits there, fading, taking up the same space as something real.
The loudest idea is rarely the one that survives.
